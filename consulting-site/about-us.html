Expert Clinical Psychologist Can Uzun

Can Uzun is an expert clinical psychologist, adult therapist, family and couples counselor, child and adolescent psychologist, and play therapist. Born in Çanakkale, he completed his primary education at Atatürk Primary School and his high school education at Çan H. İbrahim Bodur Anatolian High School. He began his undergraduate education in psychology at Strasbourg University in 2003, graduating with the highest honors. He then specialized in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy during his Clinical Psychology Master’s program at Strasbourg University.


During his undergraduate studies, he completed internships at :


  • Haydarpaşa GATA Hospital Adult Psychiatry Service,
  • Bakırköy Psychiatric and Neurological Diseases Hospital’s Child-Adolescent and Adult Psychiatry Clinics and Services,
  • Strasbourg University Adult and Child Psychiatry Services.

For his master’s degree, he completed his child-adolescent internship and applications at Dörtçelik Children’s Hospital, and his adult internship and applications at Strasbourg University Medical Faculty Hospital.


In 2008, he worked as a psychologist and psychotechnical evaluation specialist at a private medical center in Bursa. In 2009, he was appointed as a psychologist to the Mental Health Branch of the Health Directorate. At the Mental Health Branch, he served as an instructor and coordinator for numerous training programs, especially the 0-6 Year-Old Child Psychosocial Development Support Program (ÇPGD). He conducted many interviews and informational activities for families and followed up on family-child interactions. He participated in numerous congresses and workshops on substance use prevention in children and adolescents and on child abuse. He worked in the field of disaster psychology with emergency health workers and National Medical Rescue teams and served in disaster areas.


He has received training in disaster and trauma from the Turkish Psychological Association (TPD). He completed the Family Counseling training organized by ATED (Association of Family and Community Education Counseling) in partnership with universities. He has completed Transactional Analysis (TA) and Thematic Apperception Test (TAT) training. Additionally, he received the Family Education Program Trainer Training from the Ministry of Family and Social Policies and is a certified trainer. He provides counseling to couples and families.


He has completed Child-Centered Play Therapy training and applies these techniques. He completed a second undergraduate degree in Human Resources Management. In 2012, he opened his own office at Psikolog16 Family Counseling Center, providing psychological services to clients of different age groups from Bursa and surrounding provinces, adhering to scientific and ethical principles. He is a member of the Turkish Psychological Association Bursa Branch and the Bursa Association for the Prevention and Rehabilitation of Child Abuse.


Currently, he offers psychological therapy services for children, adolescents, adults, couples, and families at his office under Uzun Counseling in Van. He is married and has one child.
